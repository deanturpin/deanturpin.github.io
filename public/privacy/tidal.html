Privacy Policy

Tidal DJ Player

Overview

Tidal DJ Player is a third-party web application that helps DJs manage their Tidal playlists with advanced sorting and organisation features.

Data We Access

When you connect your Tidal account, we request access to:

  • User account information - To display your profile
  • Playlists - To read and modify your playlists
  • Playback - To play music through our interface

How We Use Your Data

Your Tidal data is used solely to:

  • Display your playlists and tracks
  • Sort and organise your music by BPM and key
  • Play music through the Tidal streaming service

We do NOT:

  • Store your Tidal credentials on our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or analytics
  • Access your data when you're not using the app

Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No personal data is stored on our servers.

Revoking Access

You can revoke this app's access to your Tidal account at any time through your Tidal account settings at listen.tidal.com.
